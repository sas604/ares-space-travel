About Ares Space Travel

Our Story

Founded in 2040 Ares Space Travelis the biggest space tour-operator in the world, Ares owns 50% of all commercial lunch pads around the Earth and on the Moon. Ares is the only commercial space travel company that provides direct flights to Mars. Our spaceships are equipped with all the latest technologies, to provide safe and comfortable travel for our customers. Our tours are designed to create unforgettable experiences.

Our Mission

Ares Space Travel offers exclusive tours to Mars and to the Moon. Our mission is to create unique experience for our customers. We offer direct flights to any destination, complex tours are available as well.
